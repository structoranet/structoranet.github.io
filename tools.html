Simple resources for better revision.

Use these practical prompts and checklists to review clarity, structure, and purpose before submitting your work.

1

Clarity Check

Can a reader identify your main point within the opening paragraph?

2

Structure Check

Does every paragraph have one clear job and a logical connection to the next?

3

Evidence Check

Are claims supported with specific examples rather than general statements?

4

Final Read

Read aloud once to catch awkward rhythm, repetition, and missing words.
