About Structora

Writing support that keeps the writer in control.

Structora focuses on editing, feedback, and educational guidance—not replacing the writer.

Our approach

Good feedback should make the current draft clearer and help the writer make better decisions next time. That means explaining patterns, identifying structural problems, and preserving the writer’s own voice.

Who it is for

Structora is especially useful for adult students, career changers, ESL professionals, and applicants whose experiences do not fit a traditional template.

What Structora does not do

Structora does not position itself as a ghostwriting service. The emphasis is on ethical editing, practical feedback, and educational support.
